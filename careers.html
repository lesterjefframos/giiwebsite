Management Associate

Overview: The Management Associate Program is designed for high-potential individuals with identified leadership and management capabilities. You will be able to accelerate your professional development by fully leveraging these opportunities throughout your 24-month rotation.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assist and support the marketing team with marketing planning & research activities
  • Writing articles and promotional material for online media like social marketing and offline activities, including forums, bloggers, social media, brochures, letters, emails, etc.
  • Planning engaging written copy and content for the website
  • Reviewing the website, ensuring the website is frequently updated with the latest information
  • Updating marketing department documentation and monthly report ·Plan all web, SEO/SEM, marketing database, email, social media, and display advertising campaigns
  • Measure and report performance of all digital marketing campaigns and assess against goals (ROI and KPIs)
  • Work with designer and external advertising / PR agencies/service vendors to ensure that all the marketing collateral is on-board and eye-catching
  • Work closely with the operation team, especially the customer services department, to ensure the issues/concerns of members are resolved
  • Any other tasks that are being assigned by a superior or Management from time to time

Requirements:

  • Candidate Must possess at least a Bachelors/College Degree in Business, Marketing, or equivalent field
  • At least 1 years' experience in marketing functions or similar roles
  • Fresh graduates are encouraged to apply
  • Excellent copywriting skills and experience
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Office and Microsoft Excel to analyze data and spot trends
  • Knowledge of website analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) will be an added advantage
  • Able to multitask, excellent organizational/ time management skills
  • Positive attitude, adaptability, creativity & commercial awareness
  • Ability to work independently and act with initiative
  • Proficient in English and Chinese/Thai/Vietnamese language
